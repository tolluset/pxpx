# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Pixel Game to be a respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be direct, constructive, and specific
- Assume good faith, but challenge ideas clearly when needed
- Share context, reproduction steps, and evidence when reporting problems
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
- Doxxing, threats, or discriminatory language
- Deliberately disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository spaces and project-related communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this policy. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent participation limits.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the repository hosting platform. Include links, timestamps, and any relevant context.
